Welcome to "Marshall's Master of Management Adventure"

Welcome to "Marshall's Master of Management Adventure," an immersive game that puts you in the shoes of Marshall as he navigates through the challenges and triumphs of pursuing a Master of Management degree program at the University of Calgary. Join Marshall on his journey as he attends classes, tackles group projects, navigates internship opportunities, faces leadership challenges, and ultimately strives to graduate successfully. Your decisions will shape Marshall's path and determine his success in the world of management.

Each decision you make directly affects your overall grade. Just like in class, you need to achieve 70% or higher to pass the program. Are you ready to embark on this educational and thrilling adventure? Let's begin Marshall's Master of Management Adventure!
